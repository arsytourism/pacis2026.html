Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

Are flights to Bali, Yogyakarta or Labuan Bajo included?
Domestic flights and train tickets are not included in the package price, but we're happy to recommend the best connections from Jakarta and assist with arrangements. Jakarta has frequent direct flights to all tour destinations (Bali ±1 h 50 m, Yogyakarta ±1 h, Labuan Bajo ±2.5 h), and Bandung is just ±45 minutes away by Whoosh high-speed rail.
How do I pay, and is it safe to book via WhatsApp?
WhatsApp is simply how we talk — your booking is confirmed with a written quotation and invoice from our team. Payment options and terms are stated clearly on the invoice before you commit. Nothing is charged when you send a booking request.
Can I customize a package or combine destinations?
Absolutely. Every itinerary is fully customizable — extend nights, upgrade hotels, add destinations, or combine two packages (e.g. Bali + Labuan Bajo). Consultation is free; just tell us what you have in mind.
Do the guides speak English?
Yes. All tours for PACIS 2026 delegates are led by licensed, English-speaking guides, with private air-conditioned transport throughout the program.
Can you cater to halal, vegetarian or other dietary needs?
Yes — halal, vegetarian, and most dietary preferences can be arranged at no extra hassle. Mention it in your booking message and we'll plan the meals accordingly.
What if I'm travelling alone?
Published rates are based on groups of 2–10 with twin-sharing rooms. Solo travellers are very welcome — contact us and we'll quote your trip individually, or match you to a joining group where possible.
Are the prices final?
Rates are per person, based on group size and hotel class as shown, and may be subject to a high-season surcharge or change without prior notice. Your final price is always confirmed in writing before you pay.
